Scopes Demonstrated

Login Kit: used when the user clicks “Connect TikTok Account” and authorizes through TikTok OAuth.

user.info.basic: used to identify the connected TikTok account after authorization.

Content Posting API: used to send approved original videos from Desty to TikTok.

video.publish: used by the “Publish to TikTok” button for direct posting.

video.upload: used by the “Upload as Draft” button when the user wants to review and complete posting inside TikTok.
